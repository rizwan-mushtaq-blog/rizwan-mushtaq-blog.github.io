Project 1: Predicting Credit Card Behavior in the United States

This short paper will help to predict the credit card behavior based on demographic and household characteristics in the United States. Demographic indicators such as age, gender, marital status, financial literacy, education and profession along with household characteristics such as household income state and region of residence will be used to predict the credit card behavior. These variables play might play crucial role in determining the credit card behavior of any individual.

Project 3: Predicting Financial Literacy levels in the USA

The aim of this short paper is to predict the financial literacy levels of US households. Financial literacy is defined as the knowledge of basic financial concepts. For that purpose, I use survey data sets provided by FINRA from 2009 to 2018. I used all the surveys conducted so far that include (108310) number of households in the US. I examine whether we can predict financial literacy level of the participant based on their demographic, education and profession ?
